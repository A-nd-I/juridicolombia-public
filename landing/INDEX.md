## Core Message

JuridiColombia gives Colombian legal professionals instant, private, offline access to core legal references in Chrome.

## Key Proof Points

- Browser-native workflow.
- Offline-first embedded legal references.
- Minimal permissions.
- Search by `artículo` number or keyword.
- Official-source buttons.
- Local custom links.

## Presentation Checklist

- Open `index.html`.
- Prepare one real search example.
- Explain privacy-first positioning.
- Show official-source links.
- Clarify that the tool is reference-only, not legal advice.
- Invite feedback through GitHub Issues.
