# JuridiColombia - Product Description

## Executive Summary

JuridiColombia is a Chrome Extension for Colombian legal professionals who need fast, private, offline access to core legal references. It brings `Código Civil`, `Código de Comercio`, and `Código General del Proceso` into the browser with instant search by `artículo` number or keyword.

The product is built for practical legal work: fewer tabs, fewer slow public portals, no backend dependency for the core workflow, and minimal permissions.

## Problem

Attorneys, law students, notaries, and legal teams often lose time searching public legal portals. These portals may be slow, hard to navigate, or unavailable at the exact moment a professional needs a reference.

Common pain points:

- Repeated searches across multiple official websites.
- Slow navigation during meetings, hearings, or drafting sessions.
- Dependence on internet connectivity.
- Privacy concerns when research behavior passes through external services.
- Poor workflow integration with the browser where legal work already happens.

## Solution

JuridiColombia embeds key Colombian legal references inside a Chrome Extension. Users can search locally, navigate results, open official sources, and save their own legal links.

The core promise:

> Public legal references, available in the browser, fast and private by design.

## Current Feature Set

- Search by exact `artículo` number.
- Search by keyword across embedded legal text.
- Paginated keyword results with Previous / Next navigation.
- Official-source quick links.
- Custom saved links.
- Local-first storage.
- Clean popup and expanded-window experiences.

## Included Legal References

- `Código Civil` - Ley 84 de 1873
- `Código de Comercio` - Decreto 410 de 1971
- `Código General del Proceso` - Ley 1564 de 2012

## Technical Differentiators

- Chrome Extension MV3.
- Clean Architecture: domain, application, infrastructure, presentation.
- TypeScript for safer application code.
- Jest tests for critical paths.
- ESBuild for fast bundling.
- Embedded legal datasets for offline-first access.
- Minimal permission model focused on Chrome Storage.

## Product Differentiators

- Offline availability for the core search workflow.
- No backend required for legal-text lookup.
- Fast local search.
- User-owned saved links.
- Official-source access without forcing users to start from slow portals.
- Lightweight product surface designed for legal research moments.

JuridiColombia should be positioned as a practical legal productivity tool, not as a legal-advice platform. The strongest message is privacy-first, offline-first, fast legal reference for Colombian workflows.
