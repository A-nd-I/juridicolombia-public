JURIDICOLOMBIA - LANDING PACKAGE

Public-facing product and sales materials for JuridiColombia.

LANGUAGE STANDARD

- General product, engineering, roadmap, sales, issues, and contact copy: English.
- Colombian legal-domain names remain in Spanish when they refer to official legal concepts or sources:
  - Código Civil
  - Código de Comercio
  - Código General del Proceso
  - artículo

CORE MESSAGE

JuridiColombia gives Colombian legal professionals instant, private, offline access to core legal references in Chrome.

KEY POINTS

- Search by artículo number or keyword.
- Embedded legal references for offline use.
- Minimal permissions.
- Official-source quick links.
- User-managed custom links.
- Public repository should show screenshots, roadmap, issues, and contact.
