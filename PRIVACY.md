# Privacy Policy

JuridiColombia is designed as a privacy-first Chrome Extension.

## Core Principle

The extension should help users search public Colombian legal references without sending their legal research activity to an external server.

## Data Stored Locally

JuridiColombia may store:

- User-created custom legal links.
- Local extension preferences.
- Cached embedded legal document metadata when needed by the extension.

This data is stored through Chrome Storage in the user's browser environment.

## Data Not Collected by the Core Extension

The core extension does not intentionally collect:

- Search queries on a remote server.
- Browsing history.
- Personal legal matters.
- Credentials.
- Payment data.
- Analytics events tied to individual legal research.

## Permissions

The extension is designed to use a minimal permission model, primarily `storage`, so users can save local preferences and custom links.

## External Links

JuridiColombia includes links to official public sources, such as SUIN Juriscol, Secretaría del Senado, Función Pública, and other Colombian legal references. When users open those websites, their interaction is governed by the privacy policies of those external websites.

## Legal Disclaimer

JuridiColombia is a reference tool. It does not provide legal advice and does not replace official legal sources or professional legal counsel.
