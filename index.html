Trust model

Private by product shape, not by slogan.

JuridiColombia keeps the core workflow local: embedded references, browser storage, and official-source links.

  • Minimal permissions. The extension is designed around Chrome Storage instead of broad browser access.
  • No core backend dependency. Search does not require sending legal research queries to a server.
  • Official-source verification. Users can open public sources when they need to confirm legal text.
  • Clear boundary. The product supports reference work and does not provide legal advice.
