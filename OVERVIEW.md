## Communication Standard

All general product, engineering, roadmap, and contact copy should be written in English.

Spanish is preserved only for Colombian legal-domain terms and official legal names, for example:

- `Código Civil`
- `Código de Comercio`
- `Código General del Proceso`
- `artículo`

## Main Product Narrative

JuridiColombia is a privacy-first Chrome Extension for Colombian legal reference. It brings core legal texts into the browser, supports fast local search, and provides quick access to official sources.
