# Sales Notes - JuridiColombia

## Opening

"How much time do you lose each week looking for a specific `artículo` in Colombian legal codes across slow public portals?"

Pause and let the user answer.

"JuridiColombia reduces that friction. It is a Chrome Extension that brings key Colombian legal references directly into the browser: fast, private, and available offline."

## Problem Narrative

Legal professionals need reliable public legal references, but the workflow is often inefficient:

- Public portals can be slow.
- Users repeat the same searches often.
- Connectivity is not always reliable.
- Legal research should remain private.
- Switching between tabs breaks focus.

## Solution Narrative

JuridiColombia embeds core public legal references into a focused browser extension.

It currently supports:

- `Código Civil`
- `Código de Comercio`
- `Código General del Proceso`

Users can search by `artículo` number, search by keyword, move through multiple results, open official public sources, and save their own links.

## Differentiators

Compared with public portals:

- Faster access.
- Cleaner workflow.
- Offline availability for embedded references.
- Browser-native experience.

Compared with cloud legal tools:

- No backend required for the core search workflow.
- Minimal permissions.
- Private local storage.
- No need to send research queries to a third-party server.

## Demo Flow

1. Open the extension.
2. Search an `artículo` by number.
3. Search a keyword with multiple results.
4. Navigate results with Previous / Next.
5. Switch between `Código Civil`, `Código de Comercio`, and `Código General del Proceso`.
6. Open an official-source link.
7. Add a custom legal web page.

## Objections and Responses

### "Can I just use Google?"

Yes, but Google adds extra steps: search results, ads, source verification, multiple tabs, and tracking. JuridiColombia is designed for direct legal-reference workflows.

### "Is this legal advice?"

No. It is a reference tool. Users should verify official sources and consult licensed attorneys for legal advice.

### "What happens when legal texts change?"

Legal-data updates should be handled through the extraction and release process. Official-source links remain available for verification.

### "Is it safe?"

The product is designed around minimal permissions and local browser storage. The core search workflow does not require a backend server.

## Story Scenarios

### Attorney in a Hearing

A judge or opposing counsel references an `artículo`. The attorney opens JuridiColombia, searches instantly, and confirms the text without depending on Wi-Fi.

### Law Student Preparing for Exams

The student searches concepts and compares related `artículos` across codes without jumping between portals.

### Small Legal Firm

A team standardizes quick legal-reference access across attorneys while keeping saved links local and simple.

## Closing

"JuridiColombia is not trying to replace legal judgment. It removes friction from legal reference work. Faster access, fewer tabs, more privacy."
